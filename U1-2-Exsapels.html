Jods

Construction. Brick & Stone Mason. Industrial. Blacksmith. Service. Arborist. Motive Power. Auto Body Repairer.

Brick & Stone Masons are skilled craftspeople who build structures using bricks and stones. They carefully lay each brick or stone, creating walls, fireplaces, and other parts of buildings.

A blacksmith is a skilled craftsperson who works with hot metal. They use a forge to heat metal, then shape it with hammers and other tools. They can make things like horseshoes, gates, and decorative items.

An Arborist is a tree expert. They care for trees, pruning them, removing dead branches, and keeping them healthy. They can also help to diagnose and treat tree diseases.

An Auto Body Repairer fixes damaged car bodies. They use tools and techniques to repair dents, scratches, and other damage, making the car look new again.
